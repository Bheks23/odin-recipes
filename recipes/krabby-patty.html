Krabby Patty

Descriptions

A delicious and wonderful combo of meat, veggies and cheese resting on a fluffy bread roll.

Ingredients

  • Two pounds of ground beef
  • One large onion, finely chopped
  • Two cloves of garlic, minced
  • One teaspoon of salt
  • Half a teaspoon of black pepper
  • One cup of bread crumbs
  • Two eggs
  • One tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • One tablespoon of mustard
  • Eight hamburger buns
  • Four tablespoons of butter
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced pickles

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, chopped onion, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, bread crumbs, eggs, Worcestershire sauce, and mustard. Mix well.
  2. Divide the mixture into eight portions and shape each portion into a patty that is about three-quarters of an inch thick.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Melt two tablespoons of butter in the skillet.
  4. Add the patties to the skillet and cook for four to five minutes on each side, or until they are browned and cooked through.
  5. Remove the patties from the skillet and set them aside.
  6. Wipe the skillet clean and return it to the stove. Melt the remaining two tablespoons of butter in the skillet.
  7. Toast the hamburger buns in the skillet until they are golden brown.
  8. Assemble the Krabby Patties by placing a lettuce leaf, a tomato slice, and a pickle slice on the bottom half of each toasted bun. Top each with a cooked patty, and then add the top half of the bun.
  9. Serve the Krabby Patties immediately, with additional toppings as desired.
